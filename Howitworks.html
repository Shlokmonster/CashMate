🔐 Our Verification Process

Identity Verification

We verify your identity using government-issued documents like Aadhar and PAN cards to ensure authenticity.

Bank Account Verification

Your bank account is verified through micro-deposits and account statement analysis for financial credibility.

Employment Verification

We verify your employment status and income through salary slips, employment letters, and HR verification.

Social Verification

Community vouches from friends and family add an extra layer of trust and credibility to your profile.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the loan approval process take?

The loan approval process typically takes 24-48 hours after all documents are verified and community vouches are received. Emergency loans can be processed within 6-12 hours.

What documents are required for KYC?

You need to provide Aadhar card, PAN card, bank account statements (3 months), salary slips (3 months), and employment verification letter from your employer.

How does the community vouching system work?

You can invite friends and family to vouch for your credibility. They verify your identity and character through the app, which increases lender confidence and improves your loan approval chances.

What are the interest rates for loans?

Interest rates vary from 8-18% annually based on your credit profile, loan amount, tenure, and risk assessment. Better profiles get lower interest rates.

Is my money safe as a lender?

Yes, we have multiple safety measures including borrower verification, credit scoring, insurance coverage, and legal recovery processes to protect lender investments.
